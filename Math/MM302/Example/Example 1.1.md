$$
\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!}x^n
$$
In this example our coefficient is $\frac{1}{n!}$.Taking the [[Ratio Test]] we find:$$
R=\lim_{ n \to \infty } |\frac{a_{n}}{a_{n+1}}|
